About Me

I'm a math and tech nerd with a knack for solving problems, enthusiasm for automating tasks, and a passion for coming up with innovative ideas.

More About Myself . . .

I was fortunate to find that my training in mathematics has made me an effective programmer! Both fields require finding creative solutions to a problem or goal. Moreover, the two can often be refined down to logical and procedural steps.

Lately, I've been leveraging Python's powerful data analytics toolbox with the universal compatibility of web applications. Exploring more of the capabilites of the language, I've also dove into Python's web development landscape. After a fair amount of dabbling, I found Flask and Django to be refreshing frameworks to use, especially after mainly using JavaScript based technologies in the past.

I'm currently participating in Kaggle's 30 Days of Machine Learning, a project designed to teach even newcomers how to apply machine learning to real world data.
